MD DaVis
========

MD DaVis is a tool to easily create useful interactive visualizations to
compare and analyze multiple molecular dynamics trajectories of proteins.
It can increase the productivity of researchers using molecular
dynamics simulations of proteins and make the analysis of such simulations accessible
to researchers outside of computational chemistry.
