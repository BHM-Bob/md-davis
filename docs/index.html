MD Davis

A Python package to perform preliminary analysis of molecular dynamics trajectories. The source code can be found at: https://github.com/djmaity/md_davis

What can MD Davis do?

  1. Create dynamic visualization of eletric field around the molecule unsing PyMOL.

  2. Create interactive plot for time seires data: root mean squared deviation (RMSD) and radius of gyration
  3. Create interactive plot of containing the following residue level properties:
    • root mean squared fluctutation (RMSF)
    • circular standard deviation of backbone dihedral angles
    • Secondary structure
    • Mean and standard deviation for the total surface electrostatic potential per residue
    • Mean and standard deviation for the mean surface electrostatic potential per residue
    It can also use an alignmet to align the residue level data from different proteins along the x-axis.
